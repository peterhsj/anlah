About Us

Divided into branch offices in July 1st, 1996,"Land Office, Keelung City", founded in April, 1952, altered its name to "Anle Land Office, Keelung City", administering the land administrative affairs of Qidu, NuanNuan, Jhongshan and Anle districts in Keelung City. Until August 31st, 2014, the number the office in charge has totaled 78,817 lands and 94,812 structures, amounting to 10440 hectares in entire area.
